What's this, then?

Diggers Beach Volleyball is a local and family run association bringing togther social and competetive volleyball players in Greensconsin for fun and fitness! There are millions of people around the world that play volleyball at least once a week. Come down and try it out, or even just relax and watch a game from our licensed bar.

Safe
Volleyball is a sport that is safe, fun and does not require extreme fitness. Since you don’t have to move around the court too fast, it's great for people who want a low-impact sport that will help them get fit without pushing them to their limit.

Competitive
For those who are a bit more competitive, the sport is fantastic because games can be really exciting and intense, but there’s always a net between you and your opponents.

Fun
Beach volleyball is suitable for people of all ages, and men and women alike. Anyone considering starting a new sport or exercise program should see a doctor first.
